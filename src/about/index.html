about me

To be honest, I didn't grow up fascinated by computers. Sure, I liked modding games and breaking things in ways developers never intended. I was always the person people sought for tech problems. But it wasn't until late in high school that I discovered my passion for actually programming and creating software. I found this interest by connecting it to my other passions: creating with imagination, building worlds, drawing, and visualizing scenes from books. Computer science became just like any other canvas for me. Except that its tangible, and what I make can be used by others! What captivated me was understanding how systems interact beneath the surface — the gritty infrastructure creating what users experience. Today, this perspective guilds most everything I build.
