Hi, I'm Nico!

Welcome to my website. The content here is centered around computing, history, and philosophy. My technical interests span machine learning and multi-agent systems, and performance-oriented backend systems. I work with Python, Go, Rust, C, and TypeScript. I enjoy building things that need to be fast and scale well.

Studies
